General Knowledge on the Use of Medicines

Proper use of drugs and medicines
Medicine can affect our body in both positive way and negative way, taking too many drug and medicine will harm our health.
Patients should take medicines prescribed by their doctor according to the dosing schedule, and make no arbitrary increase or decrease on the frequency and dosage of medication.
Overdose may lead to poisoning or even death, while underdose cannot relieve the medical conditions.
